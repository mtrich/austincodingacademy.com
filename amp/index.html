Why Austin Coding Academy

Flexible Schedule
  • In-person, part-time classes meet twice a week in the evenings, so learning to code fits your schedule
  • Our flipped classroom approach makes sure you get the most out of class
  • Unlimited tutoring gives you the option to book 1-on-1 help on your schedule
Affordable Tuition
  • Pay for your tuition course-by-course or pay for the entire program up front and save
  • Finance your tuition through Skills Fund, our primary financing partner
  • Talk with our admissions team about additional tuition options
Job Placement
  • Develop skills that employers are looking for with our practical curriculum, taught by professional developers
  • Receive 1-on-1 attention from our Director of Job Placement
  • Build your resume, practice interviews, and more in our Job Placement Course
